Application-Driven Innovation

Backed by formal R&D programs and a century of expertise

Established in 1923, the Devarsons group was conceived as an application-oriented manufacturer of dyes by the founding fathers. The company continues along this path and to bring this vision to fruition, the company is backed by a strong formal R&D program to produce various classes of dyes tailored to provide optimal performance for specific applications.

The focus of the company is to provide value-added technical services for dye usage by modifying properties of dyes to suit specific end use. Devarsons is also engaged in development and manufacture of dyes for highly evolved applications in the field of medicine, NDT and other such areas.

Special emphasis is given to product consistency so that the customer does not experience batch-to-batch deviation thus eliminating variation in the end product. Devarsons does not compromise on EHS issues or on compliance with all applicable government policies related to Safe Manufacturing practices and labour management.
